YDI Live Script Tool — User Guide
For all producers at Young Douglas Insurance

What is this tool?

Your on-screen guide for every call. Contains the complete ISL One-Call Close framework — all 6 steps, every script, and all objection responses.

Getting Started

  1. Open before dialing. Keep on a second screen or second tab.
  2. Select your call type at the top — Auto, Homeowner, Old Lead, or Cross-Sell. Script changes instantly.
  3. Start the timer by tapping the clock (top right).
  4. Follow the steps — tap any numbered tab to jump to it.
  5. Check off items as you complete them.

The 6 Steps

  1. Hook — Warm intro, confirm info, identify motivation, 3x "Yes"
  2. Build Value — Similarities, coverage gaps, WHY statement
  3. Multi-Line — Renters, umbrella, life/mortgage protection
  4. Cost Presentation — Name discounts, present price confidently, no pausing
  5. Ask for the Sale — Visa/MC, routing & account, or EFT. Handle objections. Ask for Google review.
  6. Wrap-Up — Cancel old policy, onboarding appointment, referrals

Objection Panel

Right side of screen. Tap any objection to see the exact response. Most active on Steps 1 and 5.

Payment Methods (Step 5)

  • Visa or Mastercard — fastest
  • Routing & Account Number — bank ACH
  • EFT — Electronic Funds Transfer, use the full EFT script

Call Coaching

Every call over 60 seconds is scored against this ISL framework. You'll receive a coaching email within 5 minutes of the call ending. Use this script consistently and your score will improve.
